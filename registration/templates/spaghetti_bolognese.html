Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 300g Minced Beef
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 2 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • 400g Canned Tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • Fresh Basil for garnish

Instructions

Cook the Spaghetti:
Boil water in a large pot, add a pinch of salt, and cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
Drain and set aside.

Prepare the Sauce:
Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
Add garlic and onion, sauté until soft.
Add ground beef (if using) and cook until browned.

Add Tomatoes & Seasonings:
Pour in the crushed tomatoes.
Season with salt, black pepper, oregano, and chili flakes.
Let it simmer for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Combine & Serve:
Add the cooked spaghetti to the sauce and mix well.
Sprinkle Parmesan cheese and fresh basil on top.
