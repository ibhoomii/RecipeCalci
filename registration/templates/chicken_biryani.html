Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 1 Onion (sliced)
  • 2 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • 1 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Ghee

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Step 2: Cook the Rice until 70% done.

Step 3: Cook the Chicken with spices and tomatoes.

Step 4: Layer the rice and chicken, cook on low heat.

Step 5: Serve hot with raita!
