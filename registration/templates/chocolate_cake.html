Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Butter (melted)
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • Pinch of Salt

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Cake Batter
Preheat oven to 175¬∞C (350¬∞F). Grease a cake pan and line it with parchment paper.
In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
Add eggs, oil, milk, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
Stir in hot water or coffee and mix until smooth.

Step 2: Bake the Cake
Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
Let the cake cool completely before frosting.

Step 3: Make the Frosting
Beat the butter until creamy.
Add powdered sugar, cocoa powder, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
Slowly add milk and continue beating until smooth and fluffy.

Step 4: Assemble & Decorate
Spread the frosting evenly over the cooled cake.
Add chocolate shavings, sprinkles, or nuts for decoration (optional).
