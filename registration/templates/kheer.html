Kheer

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Basmati Rice
  • 1 liter Milk
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Nuts (Almonds, Cashews, Pistachios)
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 1 tbsp Raisins
  • Saffron Strands (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Rice
In a heavy-bottomed pan, bring milk to a boil on medium heat.
Add soaked & drained rice and cook on low heat, stirring occasionally.
Let it simmer for 25-30 minutes until the rice is soft and the milk thickens.

Step 2: Add Sweetness & Flavor
Add sugar, crushed cardamoms, and saffron strands. Mix well.
Stir in chopped nuts and raisins. Let it simmer for another 5 minutes.

Step 3: Final Touch
Turn off the heat and add rose water for extra aroma.
Garnish with pistachios & more saffron.

Serving Suggestions

Serve warm or chilled, depending on your preference.
